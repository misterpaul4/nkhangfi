The Turkish entrance examination is organized by some of the Universities in Turkey to select students who wish to study in Turkey for as low as $150 USD to $300 USD. This exam will be conducted by the University representative and the Nkhangfi Team. This examination consist of basic learning skills test in Mathematics, Geometry & English. The best part of the exam is that it is not an exam for a pass or fail, it is an exam that will be used by the university to evaluate the students and to enable them to make the correct placement of the student in their respective department.

This examination will give you an opportunity to make your dream of studying abroad a reality with a 90% scholarship tuition wavier and also a possibility of studying for free with the 100% scholarship. With this exam you can get one month free accommodation and feeding coupled with the fact that you get quality education at a very affordable rate, low cost of living and freedom to work if you decide to do so.
